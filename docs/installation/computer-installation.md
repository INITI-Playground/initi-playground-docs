# Wiring guide

After mounting all hardware components, the next step is connecting the system correctly. Proper wiring ensures reliable communication between the sensor, projector, audio system, and the INITI Playground computer.

In the video below, you’ll find a complete step-by-step demonstration showing how to connect:

- the **INITI Playground media server**,
- the **touch sensor** and PoE injector,
- the **projector**,
- the **audio output / speakers**,
- and all required power connections.

Before powering on the system, make sure all cables are connected according to the wiring plan shown below.

<div class="center imageWidth" markdown>
![Wiring plan](assets/wiring-plan.png "Wiring plan"){ .on-glb } 
</div>

!!! tip "Installation recommendation"
    Route cables along walls or truss structures and secure them properly to prevent accidental disconnection or tripping hazards in public spaces.
